I am interested in how LLM-powered assistants can make academic systems more approachable without replacing the need for clear structure. The most promising direction for me is not just generating answers, but connecting students to the right resources quickly.

Current questions I am exploring include:

- How retrieval-augmented assistants can guide students to reliable course and campus information.
- What makes an AI support layer feel trustworthy in an educational setting.
- How interface and content structure affect whether students actually use an assistant productively.
