My recent projects keep bringing me back to the same theme: NLP becomes most meaningful when it solves real clarity problems. That includes identifying duplicated content, classifying large text datasets, and helping users understand the logic behind system outputs.

The areas I want to keep exploring are:

- Practical text similarity systems for academic integrity workflows.
- Multi-class classification pipelines that balance model performance with interpretability.
- Ways to bring NLP and product thinking closer together in user-facing educational tools.
