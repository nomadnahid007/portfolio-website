Student tools often fail for a simple reason: they keep adding pages and features without improving how students find what they actually need.

### What I think matters more

- Better organization beats more functionality when users are already overloaded.
- Search, structure, and guidance should reduce friction before any advanced feature is introduced.
- If a support assistant is added, it should help users navigate the system rather than become the system itself.

That principle shaped how I thought about BracU Diary and how I want to approach similar products in the future.
