Working on product at Pathao made one thing very clear: feedback only becomes useful when it is translated into a decision framework.

### Patterns I keep noticing

- Raw feedback is messy, but repeated pain points usually signal where product attention is needed.
- Analytics help validate whether a problem is isolated or systemic.
- Writing better user stories often leads to better implementation conversations with design and engineering.

The work is not just hearing users. It is building the discipline to interpret what matters.
